NEXUS is the ‘Inter-Branch Tech Competition’ of IIT Jammu, designed exclusively for its UG students.

Our goal is to make technology a core part of every student’s journey at IIT Jammu. This competition isn’t just a platform to display talent—it’s a gateway to bigger opportunities like Anhad and prestigious national-level tech contests. Let’s come together, explore our potential, and make this event an unforgettable experience for all!
