CP SPRINT

CP Sprint, part of Nexus '25 at IIT Jammu, is an intense 5-hour competitive programming hackathon designed to challenge participants with three rounds of reverse coding and competitive programming tasks. This event pushes problem-solving agility, logical thinking, and coding skills to the limit as participants race against time to tackle unique and complex challenges. It’s an opportunity to test your expertise, think on your feet, and compete with some of the sharpest minds, all while striving to secure your place at the top.
