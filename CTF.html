About the Event

Get ready to outwit and outplay in the thrilling Capture The Flag : Decrypt the Mystery! As part of Nexus’25, this inter-branch competition will put your problem-solving skills, creativity, and cybersecurity prowess to the ultimate test. Compete in teams, crack challenging puzzles, and conquer technical obstacles in a dynamic, engaging Capture the Flag (CTF) event. Represent your branch, prove your mettle in the cyber arena, and rise to the top as the ultimate defenders of the digital frontier. Let’s see who secures the flag and claims the glory.

You’ll need to think creatively, use your problem-solving skills, and stay sharp as you face new challenges at every step. It’s not just about winning; it’s about testing your skills, learning new tricks, and outsmarting the toughest tasks. If you love puzzles and clever challenges, this is the ultimate adventure for you!

Guidelines

  • Team size is from 2-4 members.
  • Event Duration will be 12 Hrs.
  • Inter-specialization team members are not allowed.
  • Event Mode is online.
  • Attempting to attack the host system in any unintended way by the challenger will lead to disqualification.

Rules

  • The CTF will be “Jeopardy” Style.
  • You have to find the "flag" hidden on the system based on the hints provided in the directives you will receive during the event.
  • The obtained “flag” can then be submitted for points.
  • The “flag” format will be CTF{flag}, i.e. CTF{h3ll0w0rld}.
  • Teams can use online software or resources to solve tasks.
