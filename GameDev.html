About the Event

In this competition, the participants' game development skills will be tested. They will be free to make games on any theme, using any model with a given set of instructions.

Guidelines

  • The maximum team size is 4 people per team.
  • Inter-specialization team members are not allowed.
  • Instructions for the competition will be released online on 8th January 2025. Participants will have 7 days to design and develop their game based on the provided guidelines.
  • Participants will attend an offline round on the evening of 15th January 2025. Teams will be provided with specific modifications to apply to their existing game. They must implement these changes within a 3-hour time limit and submit the updated game for evaluation.
  • Attempting to attack the host system in any unintended way by the challenger will lead to disqualification.

Rules

  • Games must be created using Unity. No other game engines are permitted.
  • Plagiarism, including copying gameplay, mechanics, or assets from other games, is strictly prohibited..
