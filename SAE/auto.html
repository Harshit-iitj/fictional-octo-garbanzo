AUTOMOBILE QUIZ

The Automobile Quiz is an exhilarating competition designed to test participants' knowledge of the automotive industry, history, technology, and brands. Participants will compete in teams and advance through a series of rounds, culminating in a thrilling head-to-head finale. The event aims to foster learning, teamwork, and enthusiasm for automobiles.
