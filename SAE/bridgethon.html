BRIDGE-A-THON

Join us for an exciting and challenging Bridgeathon event, where teams of innovators come together to design and test bridges using various materials and methods. This unique event pushes participants to think critically, and apply engineering principles to create functional, sustainable, and creative bridges capable of withstanding weight and stress tests.

Whether you're passionate about civil engineering, architecture, or simply love problem-solving in a hands-on way, the Bridgeathon offers an opportunity to showcase your skills. Teams will compete against each other in several rounds of challenges, with each team building a bridge that must meet specific criteria such as maximum load capacity, efficiency in design, and innovative solutions.
